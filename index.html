Why I'm running

I'm running the New York City Marathon in support of You Gotta Believe, an organization dedicated to finding permanent families for older youth in foster care. Their mission is simple but life-changing: every young person deserves the love, stability, and belonging of a family. On race day, I'll be carrying the names of everyone who supports this cause with me through all 26.2 miles across New York City. Every donation helps create opportunities for youth who are too often overlooked, and every mile will be a reminder of the impact we're making together.
